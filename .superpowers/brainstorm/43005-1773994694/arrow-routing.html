How should arrows be routed?

When the layout engine places elements, how should connectors look?

A

Straight lines

Direct line from element edge to element edge. Simple and fast. May cross other boxes or arrows.

B

Orthogonal (elbow) routing

Arrows bend at 90° and route around obstacles. Cleaner for architecture diagrams. The layout engine picks paths that avoid boxes.

C

Best-effort straight, fall back to elbow

Use straight lines when the path is clear. Automatically switch to orthogonal routing when a straight line would cross another element. Best of both.
